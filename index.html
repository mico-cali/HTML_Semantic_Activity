TWICE 5th World Tour 'Ready To Be

TWICE 5th World Tour 'Ready To Be' is the third world tour (labeled as their fifth) held by TWICE.


Body Section Part

About

The K-pop girl group will support their upcoming mini-album ‘Ready To Be’ by taking over stadiums and arenas their fifth world tour. It kicked off in April with two nights in Seoul, followed by shows in Australia, Japan and North America. In April, TWICE revealed the Europe and Asia legs of their ‘Ready To Be’ world tour. This month, the girl group will play three shows each in Southeast Asia, at the start and end of the month in the countries of Singapore, Thailand and the Philippines, alongside five Europe shows in between.
